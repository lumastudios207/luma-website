FAQs

How's InstaSite different from other web studios?
InstaSite stands out by offering a unique subscription model. Unlike traditional agencies that charge a large upfront sum, we provide services for a low monthly fee — covering design, development, secure hosting, and unlimited updates.
Unlimited revisions and updates! Really?
Yes. Whether it's a text change, photo update, or new blog post, our team turns requests around within two days. Note: additional fee applies for new page templates or extensive new functionality.
What are your typical turnaround times?
We design, build, and launch new websites within two weeks, including design revisions.
What if the design doesn't meet my expectations?
We offer unlimited revisions during the design phase and won't stop until you're happy with the results.
How do I request updates once the site is live?
Send an email with attachments or instructions. Updates are completed within two days. Additional fee for new page templates or extensive new functionality.
How and when do I pay?
Monthly subscription, billed at the start of each cycle. All major credit cards accepted, processed via Stripe.
How can I cancel?
Send an email to cancel. A cancellation fee applies, as outlined in the terms of service, to cover the initial design and development investment.
Who owns the rights to the site?
You own 100% of the rights to the site from the moment it goes live — content, design, and custom code.
What types of companies work with you?
Local service businesses and retail shops, from startups to established businesses needing high-quality, custom websites with ongoing support.
