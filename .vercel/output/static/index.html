FAQs

Why work with Luma instead of hiring an in-house designer?
Bringing on a full-time designer is a hefty investment. Plus, finding that unicorn designer who can juggle the whole spectrum of design projects like a full-service team is tough. And let's not forget those moments when full-time designers are just twiddling their thumbs, yet you're still footing the bill.

This is where our flexible pricing model really shines! It's way more budget-friendly, and you get to work with a whole team of designers, each a pro in their own right.
What are your typical turnaround times?
Most of the time, your design requests get done super fast, usually in just two days or less. However, larger projects such as a multi-page ebook or a landing page take a bit longer. We've been known to build a small website in just two weeks!
What happens if the results don't match my expectations?
If our initial design drafts don't make you jump with joy, no sweat – we'll keep tweaking them until they do. Plus, we can loop in other talented designers from our team to spice things up with different styles. Your happiness is our priority!
How do I make design requests and provide feedback?
We run all our design magic through Asana. Just toss in all your design requests along with specs and guidelines. Once we have something to show you, share your thoughts directly in Asana or Figma comments.
What type of deliverables should I expect?
Figma is our main jam, but we're pretty chummy with Sketch and the whole Adobe suite too. Just let us know what you need, and we'll make sure to deliver your goodies in the format that suits you best.
Who owns the designs that you produce?
When it comes to copyright and ownership of the design work we produce for you, it's a straightforward deal – you own it all, 100%. No fine print.
What types of companies typically work with you?
Our bread and butter is working with tech startups and marketing agencies, but we've also had great collabs with industry titans and local service businesses.
How do we get started?
Getting the ball rolling is a breeze! All you gotta do is book an intro call with us! During our chat, we'll break down our process and have you up and running in Asana so you can start sending in design requests often within the same week!
